I am Jinghao Xu, with Zhihu username [笑颜之花](https://www.zhihu.com/people/xiao-yan-zhi-hua). I am currently an undergraduate student majoring in mathematics, with academic interests leaning toward topology-related geometry. I have studied topics such as the [Triangulation Conjecture](https://sunnylovelyday.github.io/files/%E4%B8%89%E8%A7%92%E5%89%96%E5%88%86%E7%8C%9C%E6%83%B3%E7%9A%84%E5%AD%A6%E4%B9%A0%E7%AC%94%E8%AE%B0.pdf) ([English Version](https://sunnylovelyday.github.io/files/Notes_on_the_Triangulation_conjecture.pdf)) and the [Poincaré Conjecture](https://sunnylovelyday.github.io/files/survey_on_Poincare_Conjecture5.23revised.pdf) ([English Version](https://sunnylovelyday.github.io/files/survey_on_Poincare_Conjecture_en_version.pdf)). My studies have broadly covered homology theory, differential manifolds, and Riemannian geometry. In 2025, I participated in the  S.-T.Yau College Student Mathematics Contest on Geometry and Topology, where I received an Honorable Mention award.  

My personal motto is: *“Be your own lifelong liberal arts mentor.”* As a result, I have explored a wide range of interests, including playing the harmonica, [wound dressing](https://sunnylovelyday.github.io/files/report_on_wet_healing.pdf), computer repair, swimming, sewing, and haircutting—though I am not particularly skilled in any of them. At present, I am preparing to practice the piano, and in the future, I plan to study electrical circuits and work on small electronics projects. I also greatly admire the works of some 3D modelers, as 3D animation can visualize geometric transformations—for example, the works of [璐十二 on Bilibili](https://www.bilibili.com/video/BV1384y197p8/). I hope to acquire such skills myself to develop a more solid geometric intuition.  

Now I'm proud to have filmed a whole series of graduate standard Algebraic Topology course (taught in Chinese), which can be viewed at [Bilibili](https://www.bilibili.com/video/BV1aTHUzbEXh/).

我是E_8 manifold，知乎用户[笑颜之花](https://www.zhihu.com/people/xiao-yan-zhi-hua)。目前是一名数学专业的本科生，学习兴趣是偏拓扑一些的几何，曾经读过[三角剖分猜想](https://sunnylovelyday.github.io/files/%E4%B8%89%E8%A7%92%E5%89%96%E5%88%86%E7%8C%9C%E6%83%B3%E7%9A%84%E5%AD%A6%E4%B9%A0%E7%AC%94%E8%AE%B0.pdf)、[庞加莱猜想](https://sunnylovelyday.github.io/files/survey_on_Poincare_Conjecture5.23revised.pdf)等topic。大致上学习了同调论、微分流形、黎曼几何的知识，参加了2025年的几何与拓扑丘赛，取得了个人优胜奖。

我的一句座右铭是“当好自己终生的通识教育导师”，于是我涉猎广泛，如 口琴，[伤口包扎](https://sunnylovelyday.github.io/files/report_on_wet_healing.pdf)，电脑维修，游泳，裁缝，理发等等，但做的不精；现在正准备练习钢琴，将来会考虑学习电气电路的知识，并做一些电子小手工。我现在很羡慕一些建模师的作品，3D动画能够实现一些几何变换的可视化，比如B站用户[璐十二的作品](https://www.bilibili.com/video/BV1384y197p8/)。我很希望我也能掌握这种技能，能帮我获得更健全的几何直观。

关于影视作品的爱好，我上小学和初中时看的动画比较多，其中传播真善美的部分也部分地塑造了我的价值观。我喜欢一些洋溢青春活力的作品，比如《比宇宙更远的地方》，讲的是4名中学生克服种种困难，坐上南极科考船去南极的故事；再比如《穿越时空的少女》，女主角是一个比较“跳脱”的高中生绀野真琴(就是我主页的头像)，和两名男生的朋友关系很好，在她意外获得“time leap”的能力之后，不出所料地发生了一些有趣的故事，有情感，有尴尬，有逃避，皆是青春的五味杂陈。
